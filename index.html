About Spark N Snack

At Spark N Snack, we believe in sparking joy and creativity in every moment! Founded by Gracie and Sabreana, our mission is to deliver thoughtfully curated subscription boxes that inspire, uplift, and bring fun into everyday life. Each box is designed to reflect our playful spirit, faith, and commitment to personal growth. Join us on a journey of discovery, delight, and meaningful connections!

The Spark Box

Ignite your creativity with our signature collection of DIY projects, journaling prompts, and inspirational tools designed to help you shine.

The Spirit Box

Nurture your soul with our faith-based collection of devotionals, prayer journals, and uplifting messages to guide your spiritual growth.

The Combo Delight

The best of both worlds! Creative sparks and spiritual nourishment in one package. Includes exclusive behind-the-scenes videos of our founding story!

Why Join the Spark N Snack Community?

More than just a box, it's a monthly reminder to pause, create, and connect.

  • Curated with Purpose: Every item is hand-picked by Gracie & Sabreana to bring you joy.
  • Faith & Fun Combined: We bridge the gap between spiritual growth and creative expression.
  • Exclusive Community Access: Join a network of like-minded women supporting each other.
  • Zero Risk: Skip a month or cancel anytime. Satisfaction guaranteed!
